Add up to 3 campaign visuals in this folder and they will be embedded automatically
in the HTML version of `earning_opportunity` emails.

Recommended filenames:
- hero_work_setup.png
  man at PC / keyboard / logo-style main image
- money_bag.png
  money sack / earning visual
- beach_lifestyle.png
  person sitting on the beach with laptop

Supported formats:
- .png
- .jpg
- .jpeg
- .gif
- .webp

Preview before sending:
python3 send_test_emails.py preview --template earning_opportunity --email matteopennacchia43@gmail.com
